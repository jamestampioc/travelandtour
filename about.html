HISTORY OF THE TOWN


    Negros Oriental, a laid-back province situated in the southeastern portion of Negros Island, Visayas,
    Philippines. Surrounded on the north and west
    by its neighboring province, Negros Occidental; the province is a breathtaking mix
    of culture and modernization. Dumaguete City is the capitol of Negros Oriental.
    The government is also located in the capitol area of the City. The province is divided from Cebu
    Island by the Tañon Strait. Siquijor Island and the dive paradise
    of Apo Island lay also to the east. The northern parts of Mindanao can be seen in good conditions
    from the south of Negros Oriental.
